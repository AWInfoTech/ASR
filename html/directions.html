Driving Directions from New York City


Take either the Holland Tunnel or the Lincoln Tunnel to the New Jersey Turnpike Southbound. Take exit 14 for US I-78 heading west to exit 11. Turn left at the first circle, yielding to oncoming traffic, and again to cars from the right. Follow the circle left onto State Route 173 and continue straight until you reach American Spirits Roadhouse on the left-hand side. There is parking at the front and side of the establishment for your convenience.



Driving Directions from Central New Jersey



Take Birch Ave to Bayard Ln/State Rd. Head west toward Race St. Continue onto Race St. Turn right onto Birch Ave. Take Great Rd, Blawenburg Belle Mead Rd, E Mountain Rd, Pleasant Run Rd, ... and I-78 W/US-22 W to NJ-173 W in Bethlehem. Turn right onto Bayard Ln/State Rd. Turn left onto Mountain Ave. Turn right onto Great Rd. Continue onto Blawenburg Belle Mead Rd. Turn left onto E Mountain Rd. Turn left onto Amwell Rd. Turn right onto River Rd. Slight right to stay on River Rd. Continue onto Pleasant Run Rd. Turn right onto Old York Rd. Sharp left onto US-202 S. Slight right onto Pleasant Run Rd. Continue onto Stanton Rd. Continue onto Stanton Lebanon Rd. Turn left onto Grange Rd/Stanton Grange Rd. Sharp right onto NJ-31 N/Rte 31 N. Merge onto I-78 W/US-22 W via the ramp on the left to Phillipsburg. Take exit 11 for New Jersey 173 toward Pattenburg/W Portal. Merge onto NJ-173 W. At the traffic circle, take the 1st exit and stay on NJ-173 W.



Driving Directions from Philadelphia



Follow I-95 N to County Rte 579/Bear Tavern Rd in Ewing Township. Take exit 2 from I-95 N. Head south on PA-611 S toward S Penn Square. Turn right onto N Broad St. Turn right onto the Interstate 676 E/U.S. 30 E ramp to Interstate 95. Merge onto I-676/U.S. 30 E. Take the exit on the left toward Trenton. Merge onto I-95 N. Take exit 2 toward West Trenton. Take Trenton Harbourton Rd, Harbourton Rocktown Rd, NJ-31 N, Easton Trenton Turnpike, ... and Baptist Church Rd to NJ-173 W in Bethlehem. Turn left onto County Rte 579/Bear Tavern Rd. Turn right onto Bear Tavern Rd/Jacobs Creek Rd. Slight left onto Bear Tavern Rd/Trenton Harbourton Rd. Continue onto Harbourton Rocktown Rd. Continue onto Linvale Harbourton Rd. Turn left onto NJ-31 N. Continue onto John Ringo Rd. Merge onto NJ-179 N. Slight left onto State Rte 579/John Ringo Rd. Continue onto Easton Trenton Turnpike. Turn left onto Easton Trenton Turnpike/Sergeantsville Rd/Sergeantsville Flemington Rd. Turn right onto Easton Trenton Turnpike. Continue onto Croton Rd. Turn left onto Quakertown Rd Turn right onto Pittstown Rd. Turn left onto Bloomsbury Rd. Slight right onto Baptist Church Rd. Turn left onto Baptist Church Rd/I-78 Frontage Rd. Turn right toward NJ-173 W. At the traffic circle, take the 2nd exit onto NJ-173 W.
