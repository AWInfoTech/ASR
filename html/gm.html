general manager

René Rigau, our General Manager, will attend to every detail during your visit. René joins American Spirits Roadhouse after a long history in the restaurant, bar, nightlife, and entertainment industries. He brings decades of experience, having owned establishments in New York City, and two nightclubs in Miami. His passion for creating a comfortable and inviting ambiance, his contagious laughter and sheer commitment to providing an unparalleled level of customer service, will help our guests feel welcome and make American Spirits Roadhouse a favorite in the area.
