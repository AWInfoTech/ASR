our hero, our chef

We are very fortunate to have talented chefs prepare dishes that continue to please our patrons. Under the direction of Pete D'Costa, Executive Chef Geoffrey Thomas is taking over the reins to breathe life into what Marie and Pete D'Costa envisioned, sourcing locally, and using the freshest ingredients of our area, specializing in classic and exquisitely-prepared American dishes.Geoffrey brings an adventurous spirit to the eye and palate with color, texture, and flavor creating an all-sensory experience that melds well with that of the community in which the history of the former Landslide Saloon is so deeply embedded and which American Spirits Roadhouse is proud to carry forward. His ability to create visually exciting dishes that are intertwined with his spirit of adventure to fulfill the D'Costa's culinary goals, are an unbeatable combination.

He has brought this love of food, the community, and local sustainable farmers, into American Spirits Roadhouse and its clientele.  Geoffrey is a consummate professional who is always looking for a new challenge, and has found himself immersed in American Spirits Roadhouse's unique vision of creating a truly exceptional culinary experience for its patrons by choosing the best classic American fare and kicking it up a notch, making each dish visually appealing, enveloped in an inviting environment where everyone is welcome. He looks forward to continuing the tradition of simple and really great food.
